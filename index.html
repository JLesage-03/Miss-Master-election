Election Rules

To ensure a fair and transparent election process, please follow these rules:

Eligibility

Only registered NFONAP students are eligible to vote and participate as candidates.

One Vote Per Email

Each voter can vote only once per category using a valid email address.

Voting Deadline

Voting closes on December 13, 2025 at 1:00 PM sharp. No votes will be accepted after this time.

Fair Voting

Any attempt to manipulate votes will result in disqualification of the candidate.
